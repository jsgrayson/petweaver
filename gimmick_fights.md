# Gimmick Fights & Special Mechanics

## 1. Required Survival / Immunity Gimmicks

| Tamer / Encounter | Pet | Special Effect / Mechanic | Counter Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rocko Needs a Shave** | **Rocko** (ID 98572) | **Damage Immunity** – Immune to all damage for **10 rounds**, then dies instantly. | Use pets with **Dodge**, **Burrow**, and healing abilities to stall for 10 rounds. |
| Gorespine (Pet Dungeon) | Gorespine | **Gore Stacks** – Damage‑over‑time stacks increase each round. | High burst damage or constant damage‑reduction rotation to survive. |
| Wrathion (Celestial Tourn.) | Dah'da | **Life Exchange** – Swaps health percentages with its target periodically. | Use a high‑health pet or instantly swap in a low‑health pet. |

## 2. Required Healing / Burst Checks

| Tamer / Encounter | Pet | Special Effect / Mechanic | Counter Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Impossible Boss (Pet Dungeon) | Various | **Massive Healing** – Heals entire team every few turns. | High burst damage or abilities that reduce healing received (e.g., **Plague Touch**). |
| Seeker Zusshi (Pandaria) | Diamond | **Stalling + Strong Defense** – Uses abilities like **Shell Shield** reducing damage for 3 rounds. | Use DoTs or avoidance/stunning moves during shield downtime. |
| Morulu the Elder (Pandaria) | Gnasher | **Massive Self‑Heal** – Uses **Feed** to heal large amount. | Burst damage or block/stun the heal turn. |

## 3. Permanent Battlefield Effects

| Encounter | Effect | Counter Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Flowing Pandaren Spirit** (Pandaria) | **Tidal Wave** – Increases Aquatic damage by 25%, water-based healing. | Use Aquatic pets to benefit, or Magic pets to counter. Change weather to neutralize. |
| **Burning Pandaren Spirit** (Pandaria) | **Scorched Earth** – Increases Elemental damage, applies DoT to all pets. | Use Elemental pets to benefit, or bring strong healing/shields. |
| **Whispering Pandaren Spirit** (Pandaria) | **Call Darkness** – Reduces healing by 50%, increases hit chance. | Use high-damage burst, avoid relying on healing. Undead pets benefit from darkness. |
| **Thundering Pandaren Spirit** (Pandaria) | **Call Lightning** – Increases damage from abilities and applies DoT. | Use weather-changing abilities or pets with strong defensive cooldowns. |
| Shadowlands Encounters (Example) | **Aura of Undeath** – All pets count as **Undead** for the duration. | Use pets with strong **Humanoid** abilities (Undead is weak to Humanoid). |

*These entries capture the key gimmick fights that require special AI handling (immunity, stacking DoTs, healing bursts, and zone effects).*
